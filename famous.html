King Fahd International Airport

King Fahad International Airport (IATA: DMM - OACI: OEDF) its importance comes from being the Kingdom’s eastern gateway with a design that combines modern shapes with Islamic overtones. It is also considered the world’s largest by land area, with a whopping 776 square kilometers of land within its zone.



King Abdul Aziz Seaport

A modern port complex, known as the King Abdul Aziz Seaport, was built at Dammam to handle non-oil shipping. The Dammam-Riyadh railway and highways connect the port to points throughout the Kingdom.



Kind Fahd University of Petroleum and Minerals

Several modern primary and secondary schools provide all residents of the Dammam area with access to free education. The region boasts several colleges and is also the site of one of the most modern universities in the Middle East.
