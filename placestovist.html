Khobar

Khobar is one of the very small cities in the Eastern Province of Saudi Arabia. However, it is one of the fastest growing cities in Saudi Arabia that have been attracting many tourists from around the country and even from many countries worldwide.





Corniche of Dammam

The Corniche of Dammam is a nice place for visitors from the eastern region or from other provinces and regions. It is also a tourist destination for many of the Gulf people who find in this Corniche a beautiful break that attracts them.
